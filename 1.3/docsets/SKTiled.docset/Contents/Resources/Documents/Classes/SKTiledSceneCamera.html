SKTiledSceneCamera

public class SKTiledSceneCamera : SKCameraNode
extension SKTiledSceneCamera: TiledCustomReflectableType

Overview

Custom scene camera that responds to finger gestures and mouse events.

The SKTiledSceneCamera is a custom camera meant to be used with a scene conforming to the TiledSceneDelegate protocol. The camera defines a position in the scene to render the scene from, with a reference to the TiledSceneDelegate.worldNode to interact with tile maps.
